Our History

  • How We Started: We started with homeservice pet grooming when we didn't have any money to start our grooming shop.
  • Why We Started: The passion for pet grooming and the desire to offer quality services led to the establishment of Bath of Paws.
  • Second Branch: The decision to open a second branch was a joint plan since the beginning of home service grooming during the pandemic.
  • First Store Location: We started our business in Brgy. Rizal, Makati City.

Vision and Mission

Mission: To provide excellent pet care and premium services, ensuring peace of mind and security. Our goal is to be the preferred choice for alternative pet grooming care services.

Vision: To be the best choice for our clients' pets, achieved through the dedication of our professional, knowledgeable, and experienced staff, providing added value to our services.
