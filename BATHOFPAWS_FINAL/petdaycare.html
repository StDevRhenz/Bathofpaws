PET DAYCARE

Hi there, dog lovers!

While you’re away on your vacation, give your fur baby a treat of their own. Bringing your pet to Bath of Paws Grooming Services is like sending them on a sleepover. You may rest easy knowing your beloved fur baby is in good hands when they’re surrounded by friends and loving adults, since we are familiar with what pets enjoy. Your pet will get all the extra love and care they need, from belly massages to tender embraces before bed.

INCLUSIONS

BATH AND BLOW DRY

Our pets can get baths and blow dries while leaving comfortable on their own areas, helping to keep them feeling good and looking neat.

24/7 MONITORING

We take great care of our dogs, making sure they receive the required attention and are never left unattended

PET BOWL FOR FOODS AND DRINK

We provide our pets' fundamental needs, such as giving them food and water bowls, while we also make sure they eat enough to stay energetic and have the necessary supplies for their specific nutritional needs

VIDEO UPDATE

We completely understand if our valued customers have been greatly missing their dogs, and we promise to keep you updated via Viber or Messenger.

PET SOCIALIZATION

To improve their mood and provide them with the greatest time possible while keeping them safe, our dogs are allowed to play and socialise with their other furry friends (if approved by their furparents)
